Football

Rules, Strategies, and League Format

There are 32 teams in the NFL. Each game has four 15-minute quarters where points can be scored with touchdowns (7 points), field goals (3 points), or safeties (2 points).

Basketball

Rules, Strategies, and League Format:

There are 30 teams in the NBA. Each game has four 12-minute quarters where points can be scored with longer distance 3-point shots, shorter distance 2-point shots, or 1-point free throws.
